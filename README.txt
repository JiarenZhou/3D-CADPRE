The processed datasets and source code supporting this study are publicly available at https://github.com/ZJR-cau/3D-CADPRE. They may be freely used, shared, and cited by any individual or organization, provided that appropriate attribution is given to the original source. Because the complete raw dataset (including UAV multispectral imagery, thermal imagery, and LiDAR point clouds) exceeds several hundred GBs, it is not hosted in the online repository. Researchers interested in obtaining the raw data are welcome to contact us to arrange an appropriate data transfer method.
