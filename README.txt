The processed datasets and source code supporting this study are publicly available at https://github.com/JiarenZhou/3D-CADPRE. The complete raw dataset, including UAV multispectral imagery, thermal imagery, and LiDAR point clouds, comprises several hundred gigabytes and therefore cannot be conveniently distributed through an online repository. Researchers wishing to obtain the raw data may contact Prof. Minjuan Wang (minjuan@cau.edu.cn) to arrange an appropriate data transfer method.
